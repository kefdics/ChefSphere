About Chef's Sphere

Chef's Sphere was founded to celebrate culinary craft and to connect talented chefs with clients and students. Our mission is to raise standards in culinary events through quality, transparency, and collaboration.

What we offer

  • Verified chef profiles and tiered hierarchy (Apprentice → Line Cook → Chef de Partie → Sous Chef → Head Chef → Executive Chef)
  • Event booking with clear scope and pricing fields
  • Job listings filtered by chef level
  • Community forum for recipe swaps, technique discussions, and mentorship
  • Student applications for partner schools
